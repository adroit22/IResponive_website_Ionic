Discover your wild side!!!

The New York Zoo is part of an effort to save wildlife that began 120 years ago. Our conservationists work in forests, deserts, mountains, plains, and oceans across the globe. When you visit the New York Zoo, we hope that you will be amazed by the wildlife you encounter, and inspired to help protect our earth for future generations of humans and wildlife.
